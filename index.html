Welcome!

The QuOTh-QI group, Feb. 2024 (R-L): Deepesh Singh, Sayantan Das, Nibedita Swain, Achintya Sajeedran, Antoni Skoufris (?), Nicholas Zaunders, Cassie Bowie, Kaerin Gardner, Prof. Timothy C. Ralph, Hunter Skalij, Dr. Ryan Marshman.

The quantum information and quantum optics research group, spearheaded by CQC2T Node Director Professor Timothy C. Ralph, is Australia's foremost institute for the research of theoretical quantum information in quantum optics, covering computation, communication, relativistic quantum information and theoretical optics.

Interests

We investigate the use of quantum optical systems for quantum information applications and fundamentals.
